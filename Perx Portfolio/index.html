About me

Perx, also known as Krex F. Simsiman, is a Filipino Valorant player born on June 5, 2005. He's a prominent figure in the Southeast Asian Valorant scene, known for his consistent performance and contributions to several teams. Throughout his career, he's competed in approximately 14 tournaments, achieving impressive results including multiple top-three finishes and securing several first-place victories, earning him around $6,000 in prize money. His biggest achievement is winning the VALORANT Champions Tour 2024 Philippines Split 1. He currently plays for Paper Rex (PRX), a top-tier team in the global Valorant rankings.
